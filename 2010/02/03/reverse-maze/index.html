Reverse Maze is here!

February 3, 2010 at 8:06 pm | Reverse Maze

We are glad to announce that our second game, Reverse Maze, has been approved by Apple and is ripe for the picking. Check it out on the app store!

Reverse Maze is a maze puzzler where all controls are backwards. Your goal is to get from the starting point to the exit without touching any walls or objects. What may appear as a simple task at first soon turns into frustrating yet fun gameplay. As you start to master the art of thinking backwards the game will throw more obstacles in your path by adding moving objects, traps and control modifiers to each level.

Reverse maze comes with 48 levels, stat tracking and online high scores.

Reiteration and re-evaluating is key to making a good game and we have taken these ideas on-board during the development of Reverse Maze. However nothing is perfect so we welcome all feedback and will endeavor to answer any queries.

We look forward to seeing you all on the high scores boards. Remember you can check out the high scores on the Reverse Maze page, just click the Reverse Maze High Scores link.

Happy Gaming!
